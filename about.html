Gratitude Journal
Version 1.0

About Gratitude Journal

Gratitude Journal is a simple tool to help you reflect on what you are grateful for each day. Regular journaling can boost mindfulness, positivity, and overall well-being. By taking a few minutes daily to note your thoughts, you reinforce positive habits and appreciation for your life.

Key Features

  • Daily Entries: Write up to three gratitude entries each day.
  • Settings: Adjust how many days of entries are kept before old ones are deleted.
  • View & Manage: Review and delete past entries anytime.
  • Offline Support: Works offline as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Important Notes

  • Browser Storage: Entries are stored locally using your browser’s storage. Clearing cache or site data will permanently delete them.
  • Automatic Deletion: Old entries (beyond your settings) are deleted automatically when the app next runs.

Installing as a PWA

You can install Gratitude Journal for quick access:

  • On Chrome, look for the "Install" icon in the address bar or menu.
  • On Android, tap “Add to Home Screen.”
  • On Windows/Linux, use the “Install” option in Chrome or Edge.
  • On iOS Safari, use “Add to Home Screen” in the Share menu.
